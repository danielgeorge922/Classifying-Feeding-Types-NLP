Educated patient that the purpose of the preemie setting is to bring her milk in and that it is normal to only get drops from the pumping. Encouraged patient to pump for stimulation at least 8 times in 24hrs and then to perform breast massage and hand expression after to obtain colostrum for infant. Patient taught breast massage and hand expression. Multiple drops of colostrum expressed easily. Patient and her husband were both able to demonstrate appropriately. Encouraged them to call RN if any additional assistance needed. They both voiced good understanding.
